Research Interests

My research addresses the issue of bridging extreme-scale sensor infrastructure, effective data analysis & system design and privacy preservation for complex cyber-physical systems in an interdisciplinary perspective. Currently, I am especially interested in the following topics:

  • Data-driven Cyber-Physical Systems

  • Privacy Computing

  • Computer Networking
